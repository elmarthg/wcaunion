About Weingart Workers Union

We are frontline staff at Weingart Center—case managers, housing navigators, QA & compliance, kitchen, facilities, security, and admin—organizing together for safer workloads, fair pay, and more respectful working conditions so residents receive consistent, dignified care.

Why unionize

A union is us, co-workers, coming together.

We’re building a real voice on the job to improve staffing, safety, training, pay, and policies. When workers have power, residents benefit.

Safer, sustainable workloads

Address chronic understaffing, burnout, and unrealistic caseloads with enforceable standards in a union contract.

Respect & due process

Ensure fair, transparent policies around discipline, evaluations, and promotions instead of arbitrary decisions.

Better care for residents

Stable, supported staff can provide more consistent, dignified care for residents in interim housing.
