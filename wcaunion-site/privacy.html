Privacy & Safety

How we treat your information

This website is run by Weingart workers for Weingart workers. We take confidentiality and safety seriously.

What we collect

When you contact us, we may collect your name, email address, and any information you choose to share in your message. We use this information only to follow up and support you.

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to questions, coordinate meetings, and keep in touch about the union campaign. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Who has access

Messages are read by members of the organizing committee and designated union staff supporting the campaign. Management at Weingart Center does not have access to this inbox.

Safety at work

If you have a safety concern about your workplace, please mention that in your message so we can prioritize follow-up. For emergencies, call 911 or the appropriate emergency services before contacting the union.

Cookies & analytics

We currently do not use third-party advertising trackers. If we add simple analytics in the future, we will update this page to explain what is collected.

Questions about privacy

If you have questions or concerns about privacy or safety related to this website, contact us at our contact page.
