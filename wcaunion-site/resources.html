OPEIU

Office and Professional Employees International Union (OPEIU) supports Weingart workers in organizing. Visit the union’s website for more information about contracts, member benefits, and campaigns.

Know your rights

Workers have the right to discuss wages, hours, and working conditions, and to support a union without retaliation. For detailed information about your rights, talk with an organizer or union representative.

Support & wellness

Working in interim housing can be emotionally demanding. Connect with coworkers, supervisors, or community resources for mental health support and debriefing. A union can help push for better access to meaningful wellness resources on the job.
